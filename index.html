IGES

♠ Initiative for Gender Equity and Sensitisation was launched on March 8, 2018,
with the support of Prof. Ramgopal Rao, Director, IIT Delhi.
♠ IGES, aims to create a safe and violence-free educational atmosphere for all, irrespective of diversities in identities of gender, sex, caste, class, ethnicity, language, race, disability and sexual orientation.
♠ IGES also advocates a zero-tolerance policy against sexual harassment.

News / Highlights

SENSITISATION WORKSHOPS

♠ Organised 33 workshops on Gender Sensitisation and Sexual Harassment with faculty, staff, security and students.
♠ Trained 1100 PhD and UG students, 79 Faculty members, 300 Staff across 15 departments and 266 Security staff.

SUPPORTING GENDER INTEGRATION

♠ IGES is developing Code of Conduct for faculty members, students and staff members, on sexual harassment at the workplace.
♠ E-module on Prevention of Sexual Harassment at the Workplace - IGES is working with 1to1help.net. The modules will be ready for use by 2020-21 academic year.
♠ Increasing visibility through EC materials for ICC

ADVOCACY

♠ Reached to over 7500 people from the IIT Delhi community through sensitisation events, workshops, need assessment and stakeholder meetings.
♠ Organised 10 events in the campus to build a gender-sensitive environment through which reached out to 5000 students.

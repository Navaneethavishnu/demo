INITIATIVE FOR GENDER EQUITY AND SENSITISATION

IGES was founded by Prof. G. Bhuvaneshwari, Prof. Amitabha Bagchi, Prof. Ravinder Kaur, Prof. Sangeeta Kohli, Prof. Shalini Gupta and Prof. Sumeet Agarwal, with support from IITD Director Prof. Ramagopal Rao.

Announcements

Reached out to 3500 people from the IIT Delhi community through sensitisation events, workshops, need assessment and stakeholder meetings.

Organised 7 events in the campus to build a gender sensitive environment through which reached out to 2270 students.

Organised 24 workshops on Gender Sensitisation and Sexual Harassment with faculty, staff, security and students.

Wikipedia Editathon of Women in STEM fields

IGES in collaboration with two organisations, Feminism in India (FII) and The Life of Sciences, organised a Wikipedia Editathon of pages dedicated to women scientists, discoverers and contributors to STEM fields.
